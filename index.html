Introduction

Battlegrounds Mobile India is a popular mobile battle royale game developed and published by Krafton.

It offers an immersive gaming experience with realistic graphics, intense gameplay, and a variety of weapons and vehicles.

History

Battlegrounds Mobile India was launched in 2021 as a rebranded version of PUBG Mobile specifically designed for the Indian market.

It quickly gained popularity among mobile gamers in India and has since become one of the most played battle royale games.
